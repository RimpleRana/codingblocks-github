Aim of Uber She++

Uber She++ aims to empower women in tech by promoting diversity, providing mentorship, and enabling skill development to bridge the gender gap in the industry.

Students Applied

For its recent iteration in 2024, the program received over 4,000 applications from prestigious engineering colleges.

Important Dates

  • Application Open: Late January 2025
  • Interview Dates: To be announced
  • Internship Period: Between May and August

To apply for the Uber She++ Internship 2025, candidates must meet the following Uber She++ eligibility requirements:

  • Computer Science, Engineering, or related Software Engineering fields.
  • 2nd and 3rd year students who identify as female, people with disabilities or LGBTQIA+.
  • Must be attending an accredited engineering college or university.

The following are preferred qualifications for candidates applying to the Uber She++ Internship 2025:

  • Currently enrolled in a full time degree program and returning to the program after the completion of the internship.
  • Ability to complete a full-time, 10-12 week internship between May and August 2025 (exact program dates will be provided at a later point in the process).
  • Excellent programming skills (e.g., C++, Java, Python).
